Privacy Policy

Daily Horoscope — Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Daily Horoscope (com.jormagu.dailyhoroscope), developed by Jorge Marchan, handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Data We Collect

Analytics & Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Analytics and Sentry to understand how the app is used and to diagnose crashes. These services may collect:

  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • App events (e.g., screens viewed, features used)
  • Crash logs and error traces

This data is aggregated and anonymous. We do not link it to your identity.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads

Notifications

If you grant permission, we send daily horoscope push notifications. We do not store your device token beyond what is required to deliver these notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Local Storage

Your preferences (favorite zodiac sign, language, saved horoscopes) are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

What We Don't Do

We do not:

  • Require you to create an account or provide personal information
  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Collect your name, email address, or location
  • Track you across other apps or websites (beyond standard AdMob behavior)

Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party SDKs, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob & Firebase
  • Sentry

Children's Privacy

Daily Horoscope is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? You can contact us via the app stores where Daily Horoscope is listed.
